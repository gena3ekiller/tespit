2. General Information

Tespit is a private, unofficial dashboard app for supported vehicles. Protecting your data is important. This Privacy Policy explains which data may be processed in connection with the app.

3. No Official Affiliation with Tesla

Tespit is not an official Tesla app. The app is not operated, sponsored, reviewed, or officially supported by Tesla. All trademarks and names belong to their respective owners.

4. Data That May Be Processed

Depending on the features you use and the permissions you grant, the following data may be processed:

  • Location data: for maps, navigation, and dashboard features.
  • Bluetooth/device proximity data: for local connection or proximity features.
  • Vehicle-related information: such as status, dashboard, or comfort information if you use these features in the app.
  • Media information: such as title, artist, or album if artwork or media information is looked up.
  • Purchase data: information about in-app purchases and subscriptions is processed by Apple.
  • Diagnostics: crash and diagnostic data may be provided by Apple if you have agreed to share it with developers.

5. Purpose of Processing

The data is used to provide app features, improve the app, analyze issues, enable map and dashboard features, and unlock Premium features.

6. No Advertising and No Tracking

The app does not sell personal data and does not use data for personalized advertising. Tracking across apps and websites owned by other companies does not take place unless explicitly stated in a future version.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use Apple services, such as StoreKit for in-app purchases, MapKit for maps, Keychain for secure local storage, and Apple diagnostic data. If Google Maps or other map services are integrated, those providers may apply their own privacy rules.

8. Storage

Many app data items are stored locally on your device. Data required for purchases or subscriptions is processed by Apple. The exact retention period depends on the relevant feature and system settings.

9. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing. For requests, please use the email address listed above.

10. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated if features, services, or legal requirements change.
